Our Story

Eat Your Way

Founded with a passion for sharing the vibrant flavors of Ghanaian cuisine, BoujieCooks embodies the vision of its founder, Alice Mensah Adu-Gyamfi. Drawing inspiration from childhood memories steeped in the rich culinary traditions of Ghana, Alice embarked on a journey to create a dining experience that would bring these flavors to a broader audience. Alice's background in culinary arts and her deep-rooted commitment to quality and authenticity led to the inception of BoujieCooks. From humble beginnings, BoujieCooks has grown into a beloved culinary destination known for its dedication to using fresh, locally sourced ingredients and sustainable practices.
